Overview

Purpose

My goal is to help people learn about plants. I want the website to be a reference for those who have questions about common house plants. Viewers will be able to find a plant that they have questions about, and read about it in order to learn to care for it better.

Audience

Plant lovers and those who struggle to keep plants well kept or alive.

Content

Home page

Hi, I’m Hailey and I love plants! I wanted to create a website to collect all the best information, tips and tricks for helping people become better plant caretakers. Disclaimer, this is not a form of official diagnosis for your plant, but a way for you to self-treat and get to know your plant better. This is my personal collection of plants and what I have learned about them along the way. I hope you find this information useful, interesting and helps your plants thrive. Join the community and spread the love for houseplants!

Images for the Home page

Plant Gallery

Welcome to the plant gallery! Here you will find common houseplants and descriptions to tell you all about them. We’ve got names, general information, and care tips. Remember, there are many varieties of these plants and no two look identical, so don’t forget to check the name and description to make sure this is the plant you are seeking.

Submit a Plant

So glad you found us! If you have more questions about your plant or would like to see more plants added to the gallery, feel free to contact us and see your little sprout on the screen. We love recommendations, comments, questions and learning so don’t be afraid to spread your knowledge.
